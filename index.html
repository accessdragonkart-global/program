About Dragon Kart

Dragon Kart is a 3D racing e-sport game built on the Blockchain platform, with the characters in the game taken from a Pikalong series by a Vietnamese well-known artist named Thang Fly.

Players can participate in dramatic races, and use their superior driving skills to earn various rewards and tokens from hard-earned victories.

How to play?

MARKETPLACE

Marketplace is an NFT universe where racers converge. Thousands of fabulous NFTs are waiting for you.

Character

The characters in Dragon Kart are diverse with each of which is equipped with a default skill to create an advantage in each race.

Car

The racing cars in the game are diverse in multiple categories and each type of racing car has its own characteristics.

Weapon

In Dragon Kart, players can equip weapons for their characters and race cars. All of these weapon are encrypted in NFT and traded on the marketplace.

Combo box

There are always Mystery boxes inside including weapons/characters/vehicles with different uses..
